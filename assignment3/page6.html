Emergency Funds Services

We provide emergency financial assistance to individuals and families facing unexpected crises. Below is a breakdown of our services:

1. Crisis Relief Grants

Immediate financial assistance for urgent needs such as rent, utilities, or medical bills.

  • Eligibility: Proof of crisis and income required
  • Application Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM

2. Disaster Relief

Support for individuals affected by natural disasters or other emergencies.

  • Eligibility: Proof of disaster impact required
  • Application Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM

3. Emergency Food Vouchers

Vouchers for groceries and essential supplies during times of crisis.

  • Eligibility: Proof of income and crisis required
  • Application Hours: Monday to Friday, 10 AM - 4 PM
