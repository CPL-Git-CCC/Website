Childcare Services

We provide a range of childcare services to support families in our community. Below is a breakdown of our services:

1. Free Childcare

Access to free childcare services for families in need.

  • Location: 123 Mutual Aid Street, Community City
  • Hours: Monday to Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM

2. After-School Programs

Supervised after-school activities and homework help for children.

  • Eligibility: School-aged children
  • Hours: Monday to Friday, 3 PM - 6 PM

3. Parenting Workshops

Educational workshops for parents on child development and positive parenting techniques.

  • Location: 123 Mutual Aid Street, Community City
  • Hours: By appointment only
