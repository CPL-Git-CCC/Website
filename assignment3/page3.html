Medical Aid Services

We provide a range of medical aid services to ensure everyone in our community has access to healthcare. Below is a breakdown of our services:

1. Free Clinics

Access to free medical consultations, check-ups, and basic treatments.

  • Location: 123 Mutual Aid Street, Community City
  • Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM

2. Prescription Assistance

Help with obtaining necessary medications at low or no cost.

  • Eligibility: Proof of income and prescription required
  • Application Hours: Monday to Friday, 10 AM - 4 PM

3. Mental Health Support

Counseling and therapy services for individuals dealing with mental health challenges.

  • Location: 123 Mutual Aid Street, Community City
  • Hours: By appointment only
