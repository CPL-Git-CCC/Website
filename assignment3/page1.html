Food Support Services

We offer a variety of food support services to ensure no one in our community goes hungry. Below is a breakdown of our services:

1. Community Kitchen

Join us for free hot meals served daily at our community kitchen. Everyone is welcome!

  • Location: 123 Mutual Aid Street, Community City
  • Hours: Monday to Friday, 12 PM - 3 PM

2. Grocery Distribution

We provide free groceries to families in need. Fresh produce, canned goods, and pantry staples are available.

  • Location: 123 Mutual Aid Street, Community City
  • Hours: Every Saturday, 10 AM - 2 PM

3. Meal Delivery

For those unable to visit us, we offer meal delivery services. Sign up to receive meals at your doorstep.

  • Eligibility: Seniors, disabled individuals, and those with limited mobility
  • Delivery Days: Tuesday and Thursday
